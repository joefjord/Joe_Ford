Digital Innovation Curriculum Map

Design Technology

Introductory level training in digital design software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign

Week 1Introduction to Adobe Illustrator through in-class assignments

Week 2Introduction typography manipulation through in-class assignments.

Week 3Symbol and shape building for use in logo development

Week 4Developing several advanced layout options using typography, symbols, and shapes.

Week 5Introduction to Adobe Photoshop through in-class assignments.

Week 6Selecting, compositing, and working with layers

Week 7Type effects, paint and pen tools, basic color adjustment with photographs

Week 8Retouching photographs, filter selection, saving and pre press

Week 9Edit images with precise selection control and create dramatic visual effects with Photoshop.

Week 10Introduction to Adobe InDesign through in-class assignments.

Week 11Creating multiple page documents and text, image formatting

Week 12Character and paragraph formatting, color swatch creation using spot and cmyk

Week 13Preflight, printing, and exporting files.

Week 14Final assignment using the three software programs to build a multipage layout - FINAL

Design Literacy

Introduction to design literacy, color theory, typography & composition.

Week 1Syllabus, Introductions and presentations about design thinking and the place of design literacy in the 21st Century

Week 2What is design? Introduction to iterative thinking. How the medium of distribution effects aesthetics. Content + Visual Design + Usability = UX Design.
Assignment 1: creating basic wireframes

Week 3Typography: History, Terminology, Type Anatomy & Type Families.
Assignment 2: Typeface Case Study Booklet (research a typeface).

Week 4Typography: Display Type vs. Body Type, Selecting and Combining Typefaces
Assignment 3: Blog Mockup (use Illustrator, choose only 2 typefaces that pair successfully).

Week 5Design Trends Are a Technology: How and why design trends change over time, case study of Google's 'Material Design'

Week 6Assignment 4: Mockup a web form in the 'Material Design' style using Adobe Illustrator.

Week 7Composition 1 - Understanding Proportions: Golden Ratio, Rule of Thirds, Proportions of our digital devices.
Assignment 5: Create simple shape compositions using the various proportion diagrams.

Week 8Composition 2 : Reading Direction, Depth, Guiding the Eye, Forground/Background, Dominance, Similarity, Rhythm, Texture, Direction, Contrast.

Week 9Assignment 6: Build a mockup web homepage, applying all of the principles learned so far. Must build site in 3 different sizes (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile)

Week 10Establishing Heirarchy: Grid Based Design, Using Whitespace, How Typography and Color affects Heirarchy
Assignment 7: Create 5 design variations on a blog post altering design aspects to experiment with successful heirarchy.

Week 11Color Theory: What is color? CMYK vs. RGB. Understanding RGB & RGBA notation. Understanding hexadecimal notation.

Week 12Color Theory: Color & Psychology, Color & Culture, Color Schemes
Assignment 8: Case studies - find the 5 websites you visit most often and analyse each site's use of color.

Week 13In class work on Final project

Week 14In class work on Final project
