BIO

hi, my name is joe ford. i am an artist and professor of digital design at arkansas state university. my current work is a third-person, passive and slightly bemused look at the near future, surveillance and our complicated relationship with technology. the materials and techniques that i use and the aesthetic choices i make oscillate between those of traditional home-building and house-keeping and the stuff of 21st century digital culture.
