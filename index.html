BIO

Hi, my name is Joe. I am an artist, designer, and educator who thinks deeply about our digital culture and how creatives can shape the future. I believe that collaboration across disciplines is the best way to make meaningful change. As a design educator, I emphasize a human-centered approach to help students of all ages identify and tackle problems small and large. more >

I practice what I preach. My research is highly collaborative, bringing together art, design, science, engineering, agriculture, and public health professionals to address real world issues. Research teams that I helped assemble have received nearly one million dollars in funding from State and Federal funding agencies. These funds support a wide range of ongoing research projects including efforts to reduce PM2.5 pollution from agricultural burning in rural communities to developing new tools for students with dyslexia.

I am also the founder of Cumulus Tech, a startup that leverages design thinking and emerging technologies like augmented reality, artificial intelligence, and game design to address public health, education, agriculture and tourism challenges in the Mississippi River Delta.

When I'm not doing these things I'm playing tennis and goofing off with my silly and brilliant daughter and bringing old houses back to life with my incredibly talented wife. The common thread that runs through everything I do is a passion for finding creative solutions to complex problems and making a positive impact on the world around me.
